Consensus algorithm

A blockchain consensus algorithm is the process of reaching agreement among network participants regarding the correctness of data and transactions that should be added to the blockchain.

Other Consensus Algorithms

Proof of Authority

Suitable for private blockchains or networks with a limited number of participants, where high transaction speeds and low mining costs are important.

Delegated PoS

Preferred for public blockchains where democracy and scalability are important, allowing participants to delegate their votes for choosing stakers.
